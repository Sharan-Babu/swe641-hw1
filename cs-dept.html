About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University is a vibrant community of scholars, researchers, and innovators. Committed to advancing the frontiers of computing through cutting-edge research and providing exceptional educational opportunities to students.

Department offers comprehensive programs that prepare students for leadership roles in the ever-evolving field of computer science. With state-of-the-art facilities and distinguished faculty members, we foster an environment of innovation and academic excellence.
